# Conversion Checklist

Run this checklist before finalizing layout, copy, or code.

## Offer clarity

- Is the main promise concrete and understandable in five seconds?
- Is the primary audience obvious?
- Is the next action singular and easy to spot?

## Trust and proof

- Does the interface show why the user should trust the service?
- Are proof elements specific rather than decorative?
- Is the confidence level of the copy matched to the evidence?

## Friction

- Can the user act without reading everything?
- Are forms as short as possible for the current step?
- Are sensitive asks delayed until enough trust is built?

## Sequencing

- Does reassurance appear before the highest-friction CTA?
- Does each section answer the next likely objection?
- Is there a logical path from awareness to commitment?

## Readability

- Is the screen scannable with clear headings and spacing?
- Are cards, badges, and chips used sparingly enough to preserve hierarchy?
- Does the design still feel calm on mobile?

## CTA quality

- Is the CTA outcome-based and specific?
- Is the primary CTA visually dominant?
- Are secondary CTAs clearly secondary?

## Safety

- Are claims non-deceptive?
- Are testimonials framed responsibly?
- Is privacy or discretion made visible when relevant?
